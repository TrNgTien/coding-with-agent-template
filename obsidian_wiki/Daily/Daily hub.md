# Daily hub

**Purpose:** Work **continuity**—what you did, decided, and what blocked you.

Agents: [[Daily/AGENTS]].

## Two tracks

1. **Journal** — your normal workday log at this folder’s root:
   - `YYYY-MM-DD.md`, or one rolling `Daily log.md`.
2. **Vault activity** — changes to *this Obsidian vault* (structure, graph, imported docs):
   - [[Daily/logs/Index]] → themed subfolders under `Daily/logs/` (`vault-structure/`, `obsidian/`, `project-documentation/`, …).
